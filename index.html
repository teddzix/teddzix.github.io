Death ball

A Competitive Dodgeball Arena in Roblox

Death Ball isn't just your average Roblox game. It's a thrilling, skill-based arena experience where players duke it out in intense dodgeball matches.

  • DODGE OR DIE: It's all about survival! Players wield powerful throwing balls and must dodge incoming attacks while strategically pelting opponents. Aiming, timing, and quick reflexes are key to dominating the arena.
  • ABILITIES: Death Ball isn't just about raw reflexes. Players can unlock and equip unique abilities that add strategic depth, like teleportation, shields, or even explosive throws. Think dodgeball on steroids!
  • TEAMWORK: While you can go solo, teaming up with other players brings out the true strategic potential. Coordinate plays, cover each other's backs, and unleash devastating combined attacks to claim victory.
  • LIVES ON THE LINE: Unlike classic dodgeball, Death Ball features a life system. Get hit too many times, and you're out! But don't worry, comebacks are always possible.

MORE THAN JUST GAMEPLAY

  • POLISHED ARENAS: Death Ball boasts visually stunning and atmospheric arenas, immersing you in the heat of the competition. From sleek futuristic architecture to neon-lit playing fields, it's a feast for the eyes.
  • ACTIVE COMMUNITY: With a dedicated community of players, finding matches and making friends is a breeze. Join the ranks, test your skills, and climb the leaderboards!
  • VARIETY: Whether you prefer close-quarters brawls or long-range sniping, Death Ball offers a variety of maps and game modes to keep things fresh and exciting.
